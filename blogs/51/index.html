Apr 6th, 2008 | Comments

Watching the BGBC news 24, Olympic torch relay.

As the larget broadcasting corporation, it is shame that it become so gossip, like a girl’s channel.

What’s so funny?! Or she just actually hoping the torch robbed by those people again? So she can write another "fabulous news" about China, and the "Chinese human rights".


If you are lucky and watching that live report, you should ask yourself… Is this a Olympic Game or "free tibet protesting"?!
Do you want to see the Olympic Game or "free tibet protesting"?!

Oh!! another ‘pro-tibet’ demostrator come up……..!!!".

I’ve seen this kind of thing happened so many times in the relay report. When the Chinese flags are waving, then, the camera man started to moving the camera, and searching for a "tibet flag", and finally, found one, then the reporter start to arguing about the Chinese government and Chinese human rights again…

I’m thinking, when the "tibet flag" are waving, why not move your camera a little bit, to shoot the Chinese flag, and the real Olympic spirit of Chinese people?!

Well, another funny action of BGBC when broadcasting the relay, every time the report is talking to a kind of "pro-Olympic" (the people claim the Olympic should not mix with politics) people hold the torch a minute ago, the reporter will definitely break the torch holder’s words and asking the question about the "tibet-protest" things……

Come on, BGBC, give me a break…


Clearly, the BGBC missed the point of reporting the whole Olympic Game. Why they keep showing the "pro-tibet" things, and omitting real Chinese people’s exclaim and the real Olympic spirit?


Well, some of you will say that, this is the news, and the "free media" should be like this: Finding the attraction point.
But, remember, do not mix the Olympic Game with "pro-tibet" thing.
See the title of the live report "Olympic Torch Relay"?!?! Not the "tibet-protesting relay".


Today’s BGBC’s Olympic torch relay live report give me a feeling of: "Hooray!!! Another ‘anti-China’, ‘pro-tibet’ and ‘tibet protestor’ come up!!!". What a gossip media……

Oh my god!! what a free country, and free media……

Naive and ridiculous…
